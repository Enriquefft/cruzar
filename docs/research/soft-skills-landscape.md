## What's Measurable from Voice Conversation

Supported by research:
- Communication clarity and structure
- Reasoning approach (verbal problem-solving)
- Adaptability (response to unexpected questions, pivoting)
- Persuasion/conviction (prosodic features: tone, pace, pitch)
- Empathy markers (mirroring, turn-taking patterns)
- Cognitive load indicators
- Confidence calibration

Academic reliability: NLP + audio feature fusion achieves r = 0.25-0.45 depending on trait. Extraversion and conscientiousness easiest. Agreeableness and openness harder. Conversation-level assessment (not monologue) improves ecological validity.

## The Whitespace

1. **Every major player is employer-side.** Candidates get thumbs-up/down, not a profile. No portable credential.

2. **No one combines CEFR + soft skills in one interaction.** Language testing (Duolingo, Pearson) and soft skills (HireVue, Pymetrics) are separate products from separate vendors.

3. **Supply-side soft skills validation doesn't exist.** Nobody validates candidates and gives them a portable, candidate-owned profile that they can present to employers.
