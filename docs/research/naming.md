# Naming Decision: Cruzar

**Date:** 2026-04-13
**Decision:** Company name is **Cruzar**. Domain is **cruzar.io**.

---

## Name

**Cruzar** — "to cross" in Spanish.

## Why

- Perfect thesis alignment: the company helps talent cross from local to global markets
- 2 syllables, strong phonetics (/kru-ZAR/)
- Spanish root, authentic to LatAm origin
- English speakers can pronounce and spell it immediately
- Category-agnostic: doesn't say "talent" or "place," can grow into Shape 2/3
- Verb form gives energy and forward motion
- Works in formal contexts: "UTEC ha firmado alianza con Cruzar" / "Cruzar raises $5M Series A"

## Domain

- **cruzar.io** — $38/yr. Primary domain.
- Future: acquire cruzar.com or cruzar.ai when revenue allows

## Rejected Alternatives

- Destia (destia.ai/io) — too generic, could be any company
- Travessa (travessa.ai/io) — Portuguese not Spanish, harder to spell
- Adventa (adventa.ai only) — .ai too expensive, no affordable alternatives
- 80+ other names checked — either taken or lacked the trust/scale quality needed
