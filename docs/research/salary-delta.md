# Research: Peru Graduate Salaries vs. International Remote Comp

**Date:** 2026-04-13
**Sources:** MTPE, INEI, Ponte en Carrera, Bumeran Peru, Deel Global Hiring Report 2024, Terminal LatAm Salary Guide 2024, Torre.ai, Arc.dev, Numbeo

---

## Local Peruvian Graduate Salaries

Entry-level (monthly):
- Software Engineering / CS: PEN 2,500-4,000 (USD 670-1,070)
- Industrial / Civil Engineering: PEN 2,000-3,500 (USD 535-940)
- Business / Administration: PEN 1,800-2,800 (USD 480-750)
- Education / Social Sciences: PEN 1,500-2,200 (USD 400-590)

Overall recent graduate: PEN 1,800-2,500/month (USD 480-670).
Mid-career (3-5 years): PEN 3,000-5,000 (USD 800-1,340).
INEI median formal sector income in Lima, ages 22-27: ~PEN 2,100/month.

## International Remote Salaries (LatAm talent hired by US/EU companies)

- Junior Software Engineer: USD 2,500-4,500/month (USD 30K-54K/year)
- Mid-level Software Engineer: USD 4,000-7,000/month (USD 48K-84K/year)
- Senior Software Engineer: USD 6,000-10,000+/month (USD 72K-120K+/year)
- Non-technical roles (marketing, ops, support): USD 1,500-3,500/month

LatAm engineers earn 40-60% of equivalent US salaries, 3-5x above local market rates.

## The Multiplier

| Role | Peru Local (USD/mo) | Remote Intl (USD/mo) | Multiplier |
|---|---|---|---|
| Jr. Software Eng. | 670-1,070 | 2,500-4,500 | 3-5x |
| Jr. Business/Ops | 480-750 | 1,500-2,500 | 3-4x |
| Mid-level Eng. | 1,200-1,800 | 4,000-7,000 | 3-4x |

## Cost of Living in Lima

Comfortable single-person in Miraflores/San Isidro: USD 1,000-1,500/month.
- Rent: USD 400-700
- Food: USD 250-350
- Transport: USD 50-100
- Utilities/misc: USD 150-300

Local graduate at USD 600/month: budget-constrained.
Remote worker at USD 3,000-5,000/month: saves 50-70% of income.
Lima ranks 50-60% cheaper than US cities (Numbeo 2025).

## Key Takeaway

3-5x salary multiplier is real. Peru is one of the highest-arbitrage locations in LatAm. For a mixed-major cohort (not just engineers), realistic first international placement comp is USD 18K-30K/year, still 2-4x local.
