# Cruzar — Pricing

**Date:** 2026-04-14
**Status:** Active

Canonical source for Cruzar's commercial pricing. Other docs link here; do not restate numbers. Fase definitions + thresholds: ver [company/traction.md](../../company/traction.md).

---

## Phase 1 — Student Success Fee (Primary, active)

**Quien paga:** el candidato, solo si Cruzar lo coloca.

- **Acceso al producto:** gratis hasta placement.
- **Fee:** USD 800-1500 flat al firmar offer. Calibrar en rango segun rol:
  - Rol entry-level (<$2K/mo target): $800
  - Rol mid-level ($2-4K/mo target): $1200
  - Rol senior ($4K+/mo target): $1500
- **Vencimiento:** 30 dias despues del start date del nuevo trabajo.
- **Verificacion:** offer letter + screenshot del LinkedIn update + paycheck screenshot o email de RRHH confirmando start date.
- **Instrumento legal:** Service Agreement firmado antes de activar al candidato en el funnel. Incluye consent para aplicaciones autonomas y clausula de verificacion.
- **Cobranza:** Wise/Deel si candidato cobra en USD. Transferencia interbancaria PEN si cobra local.

**Por que success fee y no ISA:** ISAs (Lambda, Microverse, Henry) fracasaron en collection y regulacion. Flat fee es simple, verificable, cobranza one-shot. Ver [business/research/revenue-model.md](../../business/research/revenue-model.md).

## Phase 2 — Employer Placement Fee (Future, activa con 2-3 placements validados)

**Quien paga:** empresa pequena/mediana que contrata al candidato.

- **Fee:** 10-15% del primer ano de comp. `Estimate:` ~USD 3K-5K por placement a comp promedio $30-40K.
- **Trigger:** candidato acepta offer via pipeline Cruzar.
- **Coexistencia con student fee:** Si empleador paga, candidato queda libre del fee (solo una parte paga por placement). Evita doble cobro.

No activar outreach B2B hasta tener 2-3 placements previos como proof.

---

## Phase 3 — University Pilot (Future, activa con 10+ placements + case study)

**Estructura:** Anchor + outcomes, capped.

- **Anchor fee:** $8K on signature. Non-refundable. Covers setup + first 4 weeks.
- **Outcomes fee:** Up to $12K across 3 milestones ($4K each):
  1. 60% readiness conversion in 8 weeks
  2. 20 students in active interview processes
  3. 6 formal offers within pilot + 30 days
- **Cap:** $20K max, $8K min.
- **Duration:** 12-week pilot, 40 students.

**Fallback (no budget):** Zero anchor en intercambio por cohort access + signed LOI para full deployment si pilot succeeds.

**Founding partner terms:** 36-month price lock, co-marketing rights, first in LatAm.

No activar pitch universitario hasta 10+ placements acumulados + 1 case study publicable.

## Rationale

Progresion estudiante -> empresa -> universidad minimiza CAC en Fase 1, escala ticket en Fase 2, abre institucional en Fase 3. Benchmarks competitivos: [business/research/revenue-model.md](../../business/research/revenue-model.md). Significant room to raise success fees / anchor tiers una vez validado cada segmento.
